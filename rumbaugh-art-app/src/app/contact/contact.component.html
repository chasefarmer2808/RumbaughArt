About the Prints

All photographic prints are scaled to fit onto standard sized archival fine art photographic paper and are printed using archival inks. The texture and surface finish of each paper is chosen for the optimum look of the finished print.

The prints are not matted and are shipped in a mailing tube for maximum protection in handling.

Prices for finished prints range in price according to the size of the paper on which they are printed. Image sizes are appropriately scaled to the paper size. The largest paper size is 17” x 22’’ and smaller prints range in size down to 11” x 14” paper. Image ratios vary and will determine the margins on each print size.

These images will also be available as dye-infused metal prints upon request. Prices will vary according to the size of the finished print.
